Pricing FAQs

Is ViServe a law firm?

No. The The The ViServe is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We provide self-help immigration document preparation services only.

What is your refund policy?

If USCIS denies your application and it is due to an error in our preparation, we will refund our service fee in full. See our Refund Policy for full details.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time from within your client dashboard and only pay the difference.

Are government (USCIS) filing fees included?

No. Our fees cover document preparation only. USCIS filing fees are separate and paid directly to USCIS.

How long does the process take?

Our preparation typically takes 30 minutes to a few hours depending on the form. USCIS processing times vary by case type.
